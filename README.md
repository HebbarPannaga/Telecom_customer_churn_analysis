# Telecom Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes telecom customer churn data using Power BI to identify key factors influencing customer attrition and customer retention. The dashboard focuses on churn patterns across customer demographics, contract types, internet services, payment methods, pricing, and customer tenure.

The analysis uncovers high-risk customer segments and provides business insights to support customer retention strategies and reduce churn.

## Key Insights & Business Outcomes

### Contract-Based Churn Analysis
- Identified that customers on month-to-month contracts contribute the highest share of churn cases, highlighting contract type as a major retention factor.

### Internet Service Analysis
- Found higher churn concentration among fiber optic users, indicating potential service quality, pricing, or competition-related issues.

### Payment Method Analysis
- Observed increased churn among customers using electronic check payment methods, suggesting lower customer engagement and retention stability.

### Pricing & Customer Retention
- Identified that churned customers tend to have higher monthly charges compared to retained customers, indicating pricing sensitivity among customer segments.

### Customer Tenure Analysis
- Found that customers with lower tenure show significantly higher churn rates, emphasizing the importance of early-stage customer retention strategies.

### Customer Demographics Analysis
- Analyzed churn behavior across demographic groups including senior citizens, dependents, and partner status to identify high-risk customer segments.
